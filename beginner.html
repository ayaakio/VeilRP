Legal Jobs

Mining

Mining is a steady legal job that allows players to gather useful materials.

  • Mining can provide materials such as iron, copper, and gold.
  • Materials can be washed after being collected.
  • Washed materials can be sold to other players or to an NPC.
  • This is a good job for players who enjoy gathering, processing, and selling resources.

Lumberjack

The lumberjack job allows players to cut and gather different types of wood.

  • The type of wood gathered can depend on your job level.
  • Wood can be sold to other players or to an NPC.
  • This job is useful for players who enjoy progression-based work.

Taxi Job

The taxi job is a reliable way to earn money while travelling around the city.

  • Taxi work offers good money and consistent jobs.
  • The job works on a progression basis.
  • You do not need to keep returning to HQ between jobs.
  • This makes it a simple and steady option for new players.

Delivery Driver

The delivery job is one of the easiest ways for new players to start earning money.

  • Collect packages from the delivery depot.
  • Deliver packages to marked locations around the city.
  • Complete deliveries efficiently to maximise earnings.
  • No special equipment or experience is required.
  • This is a great starter job for learning the city layout and making consistent income.

Pizza Delivery

Pizza delivery is a straightforward job where players deliver food to set locations.

  • Players complete 10 pizza deliveries per run.
  • Deliveries are made to set locations around the city.
  • This is a simple job for players who want easy starting income.

Fishing

Fishing is a relaxed legal activity and a good place to hang out with friends.

  • Players can fish at available fishing locations.
  • Fishing can be used as both a money-making method and a social activity.
  • Fishing tournaments may appear, allowing players to compete for rewards.
  • This is a good option for players who want a more relaxed activity.

Hunting

Hunting allows players to gather animal products from designated hunting locations.

  • Hunting locations can be found on the map.
  • Items gathered from hunting can be sold to an NPC.
  • Meat can also be sold to players, especially restaurants.
  • This job is useful for players who want to support the business economy.

Electrician Job

The electrician job involves travelling to jobs and fixing electrical boxes.

  • Players go to continuous job locations.
  • Each job involves fixing 3 electrical boxes.
  • This is a practical and simple job for new players to learn.

Paper Round

The paper round is a delivery-style job completed on a pushbike.

  • Players deliver newspapers to multiple destinations.
  • The job requires throwing newspapers accurately.
  • Do not get off the bike while completing the route.
  • This is a fun starter job with a different style compared to normal delivery work.

Garbage Job

The garbage job allows players to clean up the streets while earning money and items.

  • Players collect rubbish and clean up assigned areas.
  • Items can be gathered for completing the job.
  • Rewards may depend on the completion percentage of the job.
  • This is a solid job for new players who want simple, consistent work.
