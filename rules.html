1.0 Server Conduct

1.1 Respect and Equality

  • Treat everyone with respect at all times. Roleplay cannot be used to excuse hateful behaviour.
  • Discrimination, hate speech, or bigotry is not tolerated in-character or out-of-character.
  • Criticism is welcome to improve the server, but targeted negativity, abuse, or harassment is not.
  • Persistent negativity toward others may result in removal at staff discretion.
  • Usernames, character names, Steam names, Discord profiles, and public content linked to your account must not include slurs, impersonation, offensive references, sexual content, or troll material.

1.2 Harassment Policy

  • Harassment outside the server, including Discord DMs or social media, is not acceptable and may result in removal.
  • Targeted insults, sexual harassment, threats, stalking, or behaviour causing discomfort or harm count as harassment.
  • Off-server conduct that creates a hostile environment or undermines the community may result in removal.

1.3 Advertising

  • Advertising other servers or communities without staff approval is prohibited.
  • Any individual coming into the server to self promote must open a ticket and post what they want to promote.

1.4 Staff Interaction

  • Staff are volunteers. Abuse of staff will result in removal from the community.
  • Staff may not use their powers for personal gain.
  • If you witness any staff abusing their permissions, open a report ticket.
  • Impersonating staff will result in a permanent ban.

1.5 Ban Appeals

  • Ban and punishment appeals must be placed into a ticket through the official VeilRP Discord server.
  • Staff will collectively review the evidence and determine a response.

1.6 RP Evidence and Ticket Requirement

  • All evidence relating to a reported situation must be handed over in full.
  • All clips must be 2 minute clips with proper context.
  • Players have 48 hours to submit evidence after an incident has occurred.
  • Holding onto evidence to report later out of spite may result in denial of the report.

2.0 Game Modifications & Exploitations

2.1 Game Mechanics Abuse

  • Exploiting, cheating, or abusing game mechanics for roleplay advantage is prohibited and will result in severe punishment.
  • Any money or item duplication must be reported immediately.
  • Taking advantage of duplication or exploit abuse will result in punishment.

2.2 Third Party Modifications Policy

  • Using third-party software to gain an advantage will result in permanent removal from the server.
  • Graphic modifications such as NVE, QuantV, and Reshade that provide no advantage are acceptable.
  • Enhancements that remove textures, alter colours for advantage, or use stretched resolutions are forbidden.
  • Enhanced sound packs for footsteps, headshots, and similar advantages are not permitted.

3.0 RP Terms

3.1 FailRP

  • Breaking realistic standards for how your character should behave is FailRP.
  • No fantasy roleplay, superhuman strength, or unrealistic survival claims.
  • No using phones or radios while cuffed.
  • No breaking character and ruining immersion for others.
  • No baiting police, criminals, or civilians only to provoke a response.
  • No using out-of-character methods to bypass in-character actions.
  • No driving vehicles inside buildings unless intended for vehicle access or staff-approved events.
  • No storing or taking out vehicles during a police chase.

3.2 Value of Life (NVL)

  • You must value your character’s life and act in ways that protect it.
  • Injuries must be roleplayed seriously and realistically.
  • If unarmed against an armed player, you must prioritise survival.
  • Do not stand around spectating active shootings.

3.3 Metagaming

  • No using Discord, Twitch, streams, or other out-of-character information in roleplay.
  • Each character’s knowledge must remain separate.
  • No transferring items, money, or information between your own characters.
  • No sharing or receiving locations or identities out-of-character.
  • No identifying masked players by voice alone.

3.4 Powergaming

  • No forcing roleplay in ways that do not allow a fair response.
  • No superhuman strength or unrealistic physical actions.
  • No exploiting animations or mechanics to force outcomes.
  • No camping ladders, teleports, or exits to force unavoidable arrests or shootings.

3.5 Deathmatching

  • Random Deathmatching is attacking another player with little or no roleplay reasoning.
  • Vehicle Deathmatching is using a vehicle as a weapon when reasonable escape routes exist.

3.6 Combat Logging

  • Leaving the game during an active scene to avoid roleplay consequences is prohibited.

3.7 Consent for Graphic Content

  • Consent is required for graphic content outside standard GTA violence and humour.

3.8 General Rules

  • Do not interrupt server-wide events without prior staff approval.
  • Do not play copyrighted music publicly.
  • Prioritise roleplay interactions over combat.
  • Pocket wiping is not applicable in basic fights without roleplay reasoning.
  • Players may not use animal PEDs unless approved by staff.

4.0 Combat Rules

4.1 General Combat Rules

  • Castle Rule applies when a group is pushed and outnumbered in self-defence.
  • No combat may happen 30 minutes before or after a server restart.
  • Each side must wait 45 minutes before retaliation.

4.2 Hostage Taking

  • During an active robbery, hostages may only be held for 30 minutes before release.
  • Hostages are not to be shot simply because a robbery failed.
  • Fake hostages are prohibited.
  • Hostages must be taken through proper threats and realistic roleplay.
  • Hostages must show genuine fear for their life.
  • Police should consider hostage safety realistically.

5.0 Heists

5.1 Heist Rules

  • After completing a robbery, players must remain on scene for 15 minutes if law enforcement have not arrived.
  • Heists may not be used to bait law enforcement into shootings.
  • Players should not abuse times when law enforcement are busy to complete heists.
  • Players have a maximum of 45 minutes to complete a heist.
  • Only five criminals may be involved in a heist scene.
  • PD may have up to eight officers at a heist scene.

6.0 EMS and PD Interaction

6.1 Law Enforcement

  • Impersonation of law enforcement is not allowed unless the outfit is clearly fake and distinguishable.
  • No cop baiting, including fake 911 calls, reckless driving to provoke attention, or forced alerts.
  • Violence against PD must have clear roleplay reasoning.
  • Do not treat PD as loot boxes or random shootout targets.
  • Kidnapping EMS or LEOs requires meaningful roleplay and minimum duty numbers.
  • Corrupt police roleplay requires staff approval and must follow server guidelines.

6.2 EMS

  • Do not camp or harass EMS.
  • Do not impersonate EMS unless the outfit is clearly fake and distinguishable.
  • Remain in character while downed.
  • Executions trigger New Life Rule memory loss for specific identifiers.
  • Players must roleplay their injuries properly.
  • The hospital is a green zone and must be treated as such.

7.0 Faction Guidelines

7.1 Identity

  • Criminal organisations may hold realistic blocks they control through ownership or compound presence.
  • They may not hold entire areas of the map unrealistically.
  • Groups may not black out without proper roleplay reasoning.
  • Groups should stay within their roleplay identity and standards.
  • Players may only be in one gang at a time.

7.2 Gang Conflict

  • Groups may make one push per storm unless the opposing side retaliates.
  • Vehicles may not be used as weapons during shootings.
  • Reasoning for gang beef lasts 72 hours unless re-initiated or formalised.
  • Gang conflict must have an achievable end goal.
  • Hangarounds may not fight unless acting in self-defence after being shot.
  • Players are expected to give 2 minutes of initiation before shooting.
