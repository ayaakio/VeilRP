🌑 VeilRP Rules

Clear rules to keep the server fair, realistic, and enjoyable

🕯️ Main Server Rules

Server Conduct

  • Always treat players with respect (IC and OOC)
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or harassment in any form
  • No targeted negativity or toxic behaviour
  • Names and profiles must not contain offensive or inappropriate content

Harassment Policy

  • No harassment inside or outside the server (including DMs/social media)
  • No threats, stalking, sexual harassment, or abusive behaviour
  • Off-server actions that harm the community may result in removal

Advertising

  • No advertising other servers without staff permission
  • All promotions must go through a staff ticket

Staff Interaction

  • Staff are volunteers and must be respected
  • No abuse or harassment of staff
  • Staff abuse reports must be submitted through tickets
  • Impersonating staff = permanent ban

Ban Appeals & Reports

  • All appeals must go through the official Discord ticket system
  • Evidence must be full and unedited (minimum context required)
  • Reports must be submitted within 48 hours of the incident

Game Modifications & Exploits

  • No cheating, exploiting, or abusing game mechanics
  • No duplication of items or money
  • No mods that give unfair advantages
  • Visual mods are allowed if they provide no advantage
  • Any exploit must be reported immediately

Roleplay Terms

  • FailRP: unrealistic or immersion-breaking behaviour is not allowed
  • NVL: you must value your character’s life
  • Metagaming: no using OOC info IC
  • Powergaming: no forcing RP without fair interaction
  • Deathmatching: no killing without RP reason
  • Combat Logging: leaving to avoid RP consequences is banned

General Rules

  • No disrupting server-wide events
  • No copyright music in public spaces
  • Prioritise roleplay over combat
  • Animal PEDs not allowed unless approved

Combat Rules

  • No combat 30 minutes before/after server restart
  • 45 minute cooldown between retaliations
  • No excessive escalation or repeated revenge fights

Hostage Rules

  • Hostages can only be held for 30 minutes
  • No fake hostages
  • Hostages must be taken through proper RP
  • Hostages must show fear for their life

Heists

  • Must remain on scene 15 minutes after completion
  • No baiting law enforcement into gunfights
  • Max 45 minutes per heist
  • Criminal limit: 5 players
  • PD limit: 8 officers

EMS & PD Rules

  • No cop baiting or fake emergency calls
  • No random violence against PD/EMS
  • EMS must be treated respectfully
  • Hospital is a green zone

Faction Guidelines

  • Gangs can only control realistic territory areas
  • No map-wide control or unrealistic dominance
  • Only one gang per player
  • Gang conflict must have clear reasoning and structure
