VeilRP Rules

Structured rules for fair, realistic roleplay

Server Conduct

Respect and Equality

  • Respect all players at all times (IC and OOC)
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or bigotry
  • Criticism is allowed but not harassment or toxicity
  • Repeated negativity may result in removal
  • Usernames must not contain offensive or inappropriate content

Harassment Policy

  • No harassment inside or outside the server
  • Includes Discord, DMs, and social media
  • No stalking, threats, or sexual harassment
  • Off-server behaviour may still be punished

Advertising

  • No advertising without staff permission
  • All promotions must go through a ticket

Staff Interaction

  • Staff must be respected at all times
  • No abuse or harassment of staff
  • Staff actions are logged and monitored
  • Impersonating staff = permanent ban

Ban Appeals & Reports

  • All appeals must go through Discord tickets
  • Evidence must be full and contextual
  • Reports must be submitted within 48 hours

Game Modifications & Exploitations

Third Party Modifications Policy

  • No cheating, exploits, or unfair advantages
  • No duplication of items or money
  • Visual mods allowed if they give no advantage
  • Any exploit must be reported immediately
  • No sound or gameplay advantage mods

RP Terms

FailRP

  • Unrealistic or immersion-breaking behaviour is not allowed
  • No fantasy abilities or unrealistic actions
  • No using radios/phones while restrained
  • No baiting or breaking immersion

Value of Life (NVL)

  • You must value your character’s life
  • In dangerous situations, survival comes first
  • Fear and injury must be roleplayed realistically
  • Do not spectate active shootouts

Metagaming

  • No using Discord or OOC info in character
  • No sharing character information between accounts
  • No identifying hidden players by voice alone

Powergaming

  • No forcing RP without allowing response
  • No exploiting animations or mechanics
  • No unrealistic strength or actions

Deathmatching

  • No killing without valid RP reason
  • No random violence or random attacks
  • RP escalation must happen first

Combat Logging

  • Leaving during active RP is prohibited
  • Must remain until scene is resolved

Consent for Graphic Content

  • Graphic RP requires mutual consent

Combat Rules

General Rules

  • No combat near restart times (±30 minutes)
  • Cooldowns must be respected between fights
  • Violence must always have RP reasoning

Hostage Taking

  • Max hostage time is 30 minutes
  • No fake or forced hostage scenarios
  • Hostages must be taken with proper threats
  • Hostages must show realistic fear

Heists

  • Must remain on scene after completion if PD is absent
  • No baiting law enforcement into gunfights
  • Max duration: 45 minutes
  • Max criminals: 5
  • Max PD: 8

EMS and PD Interaction

Law Enforcement

  • No cop baiting or fake emergency calls
  • No random violence against PD
  • Gunplay must have RP justification
  • No treating PD as loot targets

EMS

  • EMS must be treated respectfully
  • No harassment or targeting EMS
  • Hospital is a green zone
  • Remain in character while downed

Faction Guidelines

Identity

  • Gangs must control realistic territory only
  • No map-wide control or unrealistic dominance
  • Players can only be in one gang
  • Gangs must have realistic identity and behaviour

Gang Conflict

  • Conflicts must have clear RP reasoning
  • No constant revenge cycles without purpose
  • Proper initiation must happen before fights
  • No unrealistic “instant war” escalation

🌑 VeilRP — Respect the rules. Respect the roleplay.
