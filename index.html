🌑 VeilRP Rules

Clear rules to keep roleplay fair and enjoyable

🕯️ Server Conduct

  • Respect all players at all times (IC & OOC)
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or harassment
  • No toxic or targeted behaviour
  • Names and profiles must be appropriate

🌌 Harassment Policy

  • No harassment inside or outside the server
  • Includes DMs, Discord, and social media
  • No threats, stalking, or sexual harassment
  • Off-server behaviour may still result in punishment

🕸️ Advertising

  • No advertising without staff permission
  • All promotions must go through a ticket

🕷️ Staff Interaction

  • Staff must be respected at all times
  • No abuse or harassment of staff
  • Report staff issues through tickets
  • Impersonating staff = permanent ban

🌙 Roleplay Rules

  • FailRP: unrealistic behaviour is not allowed
  • NVL: always value your life
  • Metagaming: no OOC info in IC
  • Powergaming: no forced RP without interaction
  • Deathmatching: no killing without RP reason
  • Combat Logging: leaving to avoid RP is banned

⚔️ Combat Rules

  • No combat 30 minutes before/after restart
  • 45 minute cooldown between retaliation fights
  • Combat must always have RP reasoning

🧷 Hostage Rules

  • Max hostage time: 30 minutes
  • No fake hostages or abuse of system
  • Hostages must be taken through RP threats
  • Must show realistic fear

💰 Heist Rules

  • Must stay 15 minutes after completion if no PD arrives
  • No baiting PD into gunfights
  • Max duration: 45 minutes
  • Max criminals: 5
  • Max PD: 8

🚓 EMS & PD Rules

  • No cop baiting or fake calls
  • No random violence against EMS/PD
  • EMS must be treated respectfully
  • Hospital is a green zone

🗡️ Faction Rules

  • No unrealistic map-wide gang control
  • One gang per player only
  • Gang conflicts must have clear RP reasoning
  • No constant revenge cycles without purpose
