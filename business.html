General Rules

Activity Requirement

  • Businesses must remain active. If inactive for 14+ days, ownership will be reviewed.
  • Owners must actively operate and sell products/services.
  • If owners are profiting without engagement, ownership may be reviewed.
  • Staff may request proof of activity (logs, roster, etc).
  • Ghost businesses may be revoked.

Ownership Responsibility

  • Owners are responsible for employee conduct.
  • Employees must follow all server rules.
  • Owners must prevent exploit abuse.
  • Poor management may result in penalties.

Economic Balance

  • Businesses must follow server economy guidelines.
  • Prices must remain within a reasonable range.
  • No artificial inflation.
  • No exploit abuse for profit.
  • Staff may review pricing if needed.

Fair Competition

  • No monopolising essential services.
  • Businesses must allow others to enter the market.
  • Blocking others is not allowed.
  • Healthy competition is allowed — sabotage is not.

Audits and Reviews

  • Staff may review businesses if issues arise.
  • Reviews may happen due to complaints, exploits, or inactivity.
  • Outcomes may include warnings, adjustments, freezes, or removal.

Menu/Product Limits

  • Businesses must follow item limits.
  • No duplication via renaming/reskinning items.
  • Custom items may require staff approval.

Other

  • Owning a business is a privilege.
  • Businesses should promote positive roleplay.
  • Ownership rewards active players.
  • Staff decisions are final.

Tier System

  • The tier system applies to food-based and small businesses.
  • Does not apply to mechanics, dealerships, or large-scale businesses.
  • Designed to control menus, reward activity, and support progression.

Tier Progression Rules

  • Businesses must show consistent activity.
  • Must demonstrate regular sales.
  • Staff may require upgrade requests.
  • Staff may downgrade tiers if rules are broken or activity drops.

Tier 3

  • Max 6 menu items.
  • Designed for food trucks and small cafes.
  • All businesses start here.
  • Must be active to progress.

Tier 2

  • Max 9 menu items.
  • Requires active operation.
  • Requires consistent sales.
  • Requires staff approval.

Tier 1

  • Expanded menu flexibility.
  • Requires strong activity.
  • Requires staff approval.
  • Tier 1 is a privilege.
